# Pipl

## Category
People OSINT / Identity Resolution

## Purpose
Pipl aggregates public records, online profiles, contact information, and digital identifiers to help resolve real-world identities from minimal input such as email addresses or names.

## Access / Where to Get It
Website: https://pipl.com  
API: https://pipl.com/dev  

## Input
Full name  
Email address  
Phone number  
Username  
Location  

## Output
Associated identities  
Email addresses  
Phone numbers  
Social profiles  
Addresses  
Employment history  

## Strengths
Strong identity correlation engine  
Excellent for email-to-person resolution  
API supports automation  
Widely used in fraud investigations  
Works well with Maltego transforms  

## Limitations
Paid service required for most features  
Some records may be outdated  
Coverage varies by region  

## Requires Account
Yes  

## Typical Use Case
You receive an email address tied to suspicious activity and need to identify the real person behind it. Pipl allows you to pivot from email to name, location, and associated accounts.

## Example

Input:  
user@example.com  

Output:

Name – John Doe  
Location – Atlanta, GA  
Linked Profiles – LinkedIn, Facebook  
Phone – +1-404-XXX-XXXX  

This confirms identity attribution and supports further investigation.

## Notes
Strong for fraud and impersonation cases.  
Best used alongside breach data and social media OSINT.  
Useful for mapping digital footprints.
